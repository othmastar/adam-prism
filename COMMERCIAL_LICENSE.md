## Why a commercial license?

Adam Prism is **dual-licensed**:

| Use case | License required | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| Personal projects, learning, research | AGPL v3 (free) | $0 |
| Internal company use (no external users) | AGPL v3 (free) | $0 |
| Modified version distributed as product | AGPL v3 (free) + publish source | $0 |
| **SaaS deployment to external users** | **Commercial license** | **$$$** |
| **Embedded in a commercial product** | **Commercial license** | **$$$** |
| **Forks sold as a service** | **Commercial license** | **$$$** |

The AGPL v3's "network use is distribution" clause is what triggers
the commercial license requirement. If you serve Adam Prism to users
over a network (the typical SaaS model), AGPL v3 requires you to
publish the complete corresponding source code of the running server
to those users. Most companies do not want to do that — hence the
commercial license.

### Q: What if my MAU grows beyond my tier?
**A:** We offer prorated upgrades. You can also buy the next tier upfront

**A:** Yes, but the AGPL v3 still applies to free users. Commercial license

### Q: Can I resell Adam Prism as part of a managed service?
**A:** Only with a commercial license. This is the classic SaaS case.

### Q: Is there a free commercial tier?
**A:** No commercial license is free. However, AGPL v3 users may use
Adam Prism commercially for **internal** purposes at no charge.

### Q: Can I contribute code and get a discount?
**A:** Yes. Active contributors (10+ merged PRs) get 50% off any tier.
Trivial fixes don't count.

### Q: What if I disagree with a license interpretation?

we prefer to talk through issues rather than litigate.
